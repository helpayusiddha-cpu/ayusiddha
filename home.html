Our Therapies · उपचार

The Five Sacred Therapies

Each treatment is a complete science in itself — drawing from thousands of years of accumulated wisdom, refined and personalised for the modern patient.

01
Panchakarma
पंचकर्म — पाच शुद्धीकरण कर्म
The crown jewel of Ayurvedic treatment. A comprehensive five-step detoxification process that removes deep-seated toxins (ama), rejuvenates tissues, and restores doshic balance at the cellular level. Customised 7, 14, or 21-day programmes available.
02
Abhyanga
अभ्यंग — सर्वांग तेल मालिश
A deeply nourishing full-body warm oil massage using medicated oils tailored to your dosha. Performed by trained therapists with precise marma point stimulation, it penetrates tissues to release tension, improve circulation, and calm the nervous system.
03
Shirodhara
शिरोधारा — मस्तक तेल धारा
A profoundly meditative therapy where a continuous stream of warm medicated oil flows gently onto the forehead. Shirodhara stills the mind, heals stress disorders, insomnia, anxiety, and migraine, and activates the body's parasympathetic state.
04
Herbal Treatments
औषधी — शास्त्रीय वनस्पती चिकित्सा
Classical Ayurvedic herbal formulations prepared from certified organic herbs — kashayas, churnas, arishtams, ghritas, and tailas — prescribed precisely based on your diagnosis, season, and digestive capacity (agni).
05
Dietary Consultation
आहार परामर्श — पथ्यापथ्य मार्गदर्शन
In Ayurveda, food is the first medicine. Our detailed ahara consultation designs a seasonal, dosha-specific diet plan — specifying ideal foods, timings, cooking methods, and incompatible combinations — as a foundation for lasting health.
