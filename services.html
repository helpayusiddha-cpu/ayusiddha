The Five Sacred Therapies

Each treatment is a complete science in itself — drawing from thousands of years of accumulated wisdom, refined and personalised for the modern patient.

01

Panchakarma

पंचकर्म — शरीर शुद्धीकरण

The ultimate Ayurvedic purification. A tailored 5-step clinical detoxification to extract deep cellular metabolic waste (Ama), reset digestive fire, and rebalance Vata, Pitta, and Kapha.

02

Abhyanga

अभ्यंग — सर्वांग तेल मालिश

A deeply nourishing, synchronous warm oil massage utilizing customized herbalised oils. Stimulates vital energy points (Marma) to encourage lymph drainage, relax muscles, and soothe nerves.

03

Shirodhara

शिरोधारा — मनःशांती चिकित्सा

A continuous stream of warm, herb-infused oil poured over the forehead (ajna chakra). Stillness for the nervous system; ideal for treating insomnia, anxiety, chronic fatigue, and migraine.

04

Herbal Therapies

औषधी वनस्पती चिकित्सा

Prescription of certified organic, premium single herbs and complex classical preparations (Kashayas, Ghritas, Arishtas) optimized for your digestion (Agni) and biological state.

05

Ahara Consult

आहार आणि जीवनशैली

In Ayurveda, food is medicine. We chart your complete seasonal diet plan, listing ideal cooking protocols, food times, and incompatible combinations (Viruddha Ahara) to anchor health.
